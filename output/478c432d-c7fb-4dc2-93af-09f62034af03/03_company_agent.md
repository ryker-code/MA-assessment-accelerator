### Narrative Summary
eBay is a mature global commerce leader currently undergoing a strategic transformation from a generalist marketplace to a 'focused' platform. Under the leadership of CEO Jamie Iannone, the company is prioritizing high-value, enthusiast-driven categories such as collectibles and luxury items, where it can leverage its authentication services to create a competitive moat.

Financially, eBay maintains a very strong balance sheet with significant cash reserves and high EBITDA margins. While top-line growth remains modest, the company is aggressively integrating generative AI to reduce friction for sellers and improve buyer conversion. The strategic focus on 'Focus Categories' aligns well with a potential buyer like GameStop, particularly in the collectibles and gaming hardware segments.
