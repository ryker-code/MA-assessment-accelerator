### Narrative Summary
The United States maintains a resilient economy with moderate GDP growth over the past five years, despite a sharp contraction in 2020 due to the pandemic. Inflation has declined from a peak of 7.0% in 2022 to 3.2% in 2024, aligning with global trends. The USD remains stable with no significant exchange rate fluctuations, supporting predictable financial planning for foreign investors. The U.S. benefits from high political stability (score: 90/100) and a top-tier ease of doing business ranking (12th globally), fostering a favorable environment for M&A activity. Corporate tax rates stand at 21% federally, with no restrictions on repatriating profits, making the U.S. an attractive destination for foreign investment. Risks include potential future inflationary pressures and geopolitical uncertainties, though the overall macroeconomic environment remains robust.
