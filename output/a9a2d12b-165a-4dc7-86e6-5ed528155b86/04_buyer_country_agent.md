### Capital Repatriation & FX
India allows outbound capital flows under the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), but requires approval from the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) and the government for certain sectors, including telecom. Dividend repatriation is permitted under India-UK Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA), typically at 15% withholding tax, though lower rates may apply for qualifying investments.
