### Narrative Summary
Bharti Airtel is a leading global telecommunications provider with a dominant position in India and significant operations in Africa. The company is currently transitioning from a traditional telco to a 'tech-co', focusing on 5G deployment, B2B enterprise services, and digital platforms. Recent strategic moves include the divestiture of tower assets (ATC TIPL) to unlock value and reduce leverage. While the company maintains strong EBITDA margins, its financial capacity for M&A is balanced against heavy CAPEX requirements for network modernization and spectrum obligations. Management is highly regarded for its ability to scale operations across diverse emerging markets.
