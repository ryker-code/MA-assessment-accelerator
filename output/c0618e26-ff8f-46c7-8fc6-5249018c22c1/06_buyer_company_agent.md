### Narrative Summary
T-Mobile is a high-growth telecommunications leader with a formidable M&A track record, most notably the successful integration of Sprint which yielded $8 billion in run-rate synergies. The company is currently pivoting from pure wireless dominance toward a broader connectivity play, targeting 18-19 million broadband customers by 2030. Financially, T-Mobile exhibits strong cash flow generation and a disciplined approach to leverage. Management is highly rated for its ability to execute complex integrations and drive operational efficiencies through digitalization and AI. Their current strategic intent focuses on 'new businesses' and broadband expansion, suggesting a preference for bolt-on acquisitions in technology, fiber, or AI-enabled services rather than another mega-merger.
