### Narrative Summary
Telenor Pakistan (PMCL) is a significant telecommunications operator currently undergoing a strategic divestment by its parent, Telenor ASA, to STC for an estimated $490 million. The company has focused its strategic efforts on expanding affordable broadband connectivity to capture growth in the Pakistani market.

Financial performance is characterized by a sharp divergence between local and hard currency metrics. While the company achieved a 17% increase in Adjusted EBITDA in local currency terms, this was offset by severe PKR devaluation, resulting in a 2% decrease in USD-denominated EBITDA. The management team, including President Mohammad Nadeem Khan, has maintained operational stability and successfully navigated a volatile macroeconomic environment to facilitate this strategic exit.
