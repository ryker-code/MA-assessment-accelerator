## Deal Rationale & Recommendation

### Decision: ❌ **NO-GO**

**Strategic Fit Score:** 8.0/10

**Revisit Timeframe:** 6-12 months

### Top 5 Decision Rationale
1. The overall risk rating is RED due to severe PKR currency volatility, where a 17% local currency EBITDA growth was erased by devaluation, resulting in a -2% USD-denominated growth (Risk Agent/Company Agent).
2. High probability of 'trapped cash' exists as the State Bank of Pakistan (SBP) maintains strict oversight and approval requirements for large foreign exchange repatriations, threatening STC's capital efficiency (Risk Agent/Country Agent).
3. Pakistan's political stability score of 35.0/100 and high inflation (12%) create an unstable macroeconomic environment that outweighs the strategic benefits of market entry (Country Agent).
4. While the strategic fit is high (Score 8.0) for STC's emerging market expansion, the SAR/USD peg means operational gains in Pakistan will likely be neutralized by FX translation drag upon consolidation (Buyer Sector Agent/Risk Agent).
5. The implied deal multiple of ~4.45x (based on the $490m reported price and $110m EBITDA) is low, reflecting the high-risk profile, but does not sufficiently mitigate the structural repatriation and geopolitical risks (Company Agent).
