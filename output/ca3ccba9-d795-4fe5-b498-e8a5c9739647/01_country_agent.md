### Narrative Summary
The U.S. economy remains a global leader with robust GDP growth post-pandemic, though growth has moderated to 2.5% in 2024. Inflation has eased to 3.5%, supported by Federal Reserve policy adjustments. The USD remains stable, bolstered by strong economic fundamentals and global demand for safe-haven assets. Political stability is high, with a strong institutional framework and a top-tier ease of doing business ranking (12th globally). Corporate tax rates at 21% are competitive, though state-level variations exist. Currency repatriation laws are favorable for foreign investors. Risks include potential interest rate volatility and geopolitical uncertainties, but the U.S. offers a stable environment for M&A activity.
