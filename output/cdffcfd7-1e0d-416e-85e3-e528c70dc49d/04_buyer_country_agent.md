### Capital Repatriation & FX
India permits outbound capital flows under the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA), requiring prior approval for certain sectors. Dividend repatriation is subject to non-resident withholding tax (NWT) at 20% unless reduced by a Double Taxation Avoidance Agreement (DTAA). The India-UK DTAA may lower withholding rates on dividends.
