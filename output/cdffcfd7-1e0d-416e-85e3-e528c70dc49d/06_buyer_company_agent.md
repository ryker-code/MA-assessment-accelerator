### Narrative Summary
Bharti Airtel is a global telecommunications leader with a dominant position in India and a significant footprint in Africa. The company is currently in a heavy investment phase focused on 5G infrastructure and digital services. While it carries substantial debt due to spectrum auctions, its strong EBITDA generation and strategic partnerships (e.g., with Singtel) provide a stable foundation. Management has a proven track record of complex international integrations, particularly in Africa, and is currently pivoting toward high-margin B2B services and digital ecosystems to drive ARPU growth.
