2. Country Agent data indicates severe macroeconomic instability, including a 24% inflation rate and a low political stability score (35/100), which creates an unacceptable risk profile for a large-scale capital deployment.

5. The valuation discrepancy between the 2023 Reuters report ($490m) and the current high-inflation environment suggests that any entry price would be highly sensitive to volatile macroeconomic swings.
