### Capital Repatriation & FX
The US generally maintains an open capital account with no restrictions on outbound investment for most jurisdictions. However, under Executive Order 14105, a 'Reverse CFIUS' framework has been implemented to restrict or require notification for outbound investments into 'countries of concern' (primarily China, Russia, Iran, and North Korea), specifically targeting critical technologies such as AI, semiconductors, and quantum computing.
