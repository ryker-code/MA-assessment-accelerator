### Narrative Summary
CoreWeave has evolved from a crypto-mining operation into a dominant specialized AI cloud provider. Its financial strategy is characterized by aggressive, debt-fueled capital expenditure, using GPUs as collateral to scale infrastructure faster than traditional hyperscalers. While currently highly leveraged, the company is projecting a shift toward positive EBITDA in 2025 as its massive clusters reach full utilization. Strategically, CoreWeave is moving up the value chain from raw compute rental (IaaS) to a managed AI platform (PaaS) to increase margins and reduce commoditization risk ahead of a projected 2025/2026 IPO.
