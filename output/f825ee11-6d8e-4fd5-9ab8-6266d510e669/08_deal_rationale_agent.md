### Decision: ✅ **GO**

### Deal Rationale
1. Strategic Fit: Coreweave's critical gap in moving from IaaS to PaaS is perfectly filled by MongoDB's Atlas Vector Search, creating a vertically integrated 'AI Utility' that combines GPU compute with the essential data layer for RAG workloads.
2. Value Creation: Significant margin expansion is achievable by migrating MongoDB Atlas workloads from third-party hyperscalers (AWS/Azure/GCP) onto Coreweave's own high-performance GPU infrastructure, drastically reducing COGS.
3. Valuation Attractiveness: A median 37.5x Forward EBITDA multiple is justified by MongoDB's strong 'Rule of 40' profile (25% growth + 21.4% EBITDA margin) and its dominant position in the NoSQL/Vector search market.
4. Risk Balance: The AMBER risk rating is manageable; MongoDB's inherent multi-cloud agnosticism provides a strategic hedge against Coreweave's extreme NVIDIA concentration risk and hardware lock-in.
5. Financial Feasibility: While the implied valuation ($13.5B - $20.2B) exceeds Coreweave's current $6B acquisition capacity, the strategic necessity justifies a structured stock-swap or the issuance of new GPU-backed debt to fund the premium.
